Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 10 min
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 200g
Vegies 150g
Noodles 200g
  1. Chop the meat into the cubes.
  2. Chop the vegies into the small pieces.
  3. Stew or fry meat with the vegies.
  4. Boil noodles and when its done, serve it all together.
